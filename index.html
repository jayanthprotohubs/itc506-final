Welcome to Lights Out!

Instructions

Click on any cell to toggle the light. The goal is to turn off all the lights. Clicking a cell will also toggle adjacent cells. Good luck!

Instructions

  1. Objective: Turn off all the lights on the game board to win.
  2. Gameplay: Clicking a light toggles it and its adjacent lights.
  3. Starting Configuration: The game board starts with random lights on.
  4. Winning Condition: You win when all lights are turned off.

Rules and Strategies:

  • Experiment with different sequences of clicks to find the optimal strategy for each level.
  • Remember that clicking a light will also affect neighboring lights.
  • Strive for efficiency, as fewer clicks will lead to a faster victory.
  • Keep track of your time and try to beat your previous records.
